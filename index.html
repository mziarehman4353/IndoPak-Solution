About Us

Our Journey in Indonesia

We first met here in Indonesia, brought together by different dreams but a shared goal—to support each other like a family. Abuzar, already a student here, became our mentor, teaching us essential survival tactics every night. With his psychology background, he became our go-to person whenever we faced challenges.

Our group consists of experts in various fields—Finance, IT, Design, Networking, and Mental Well-being. If anyone in our circle or beyond needs guidance in these areas, we're always ready to help. Moreover, we provide free scholarship guidance to those who aspire to study abroad.

We're more than just a group; we're a family.
